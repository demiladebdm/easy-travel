Cookies

EasyTravel Agency uses cookies and similar technologies to help provide, protect, and improve the EasyTravel Agency website. This policy explains how and why we use these technologies and the choices you have.

A cookie is a small data file that is transferred to your device (e.g. your phone or your computer). For example, a cookie could allow us to recognize your browser, while another could store your recently viewed properties. There are two types of cookies used on the EasyTravel Agency website: (1) “session cookies” and (2) “persistent cookies.” Session cookies normally expire when you close your browser, while persistent cookies remain on your device after you close your browser, and can be used again the next time you access the EasyTravel Agency website.

We also use other technologies with similar functionality to cookies, such as web beacons, pixels, mobile identifiers, and tracking URLs, to obtain information. For example, our email messages may contain web beacons and tracking URLs to determine whether you have opened a certain message or accessed a certain link.



Why We Uses These Technologies


We use these technologies for a number of purposes, such as:

  1. To enable, facilitate and streamline the functioning of and your access to the EasyTravel Agency website.
  2. To better understand how you navigate through and interact with the EasyTravel Agency website and to improve the EasyTravel Agency website.
  3. To serve you tailored advertising on third party websites.
  4. To monitor and analyze the performance, operation, and effectiveness of the EasyTravel Agency website and EasyTravel Agency advertisements.
  5. To enforce legal agreements that govern use of the EasyTravel Agency website.
  6. For fraud detection and prevention, and investigations.
  7. For purposes of our own customer support, analytics, research, product development, and regulatory compliance.



Third Parties


We may also allow certain business partners to place these technologies on the EasyTravel Agency website. These partners use these technologies to (1) help us analyze how you use the EasyTravel Agency website, such as by noting the third party services from which you arrived, (2) market and advertise our services to you on the EasyTravel Agency website and third party websites, (3) help us detect or prevent fraud or conduct risk assessments, or (4) collect information about your activities on the EasyTravel Agency website, other sites, and/or the ads you have clicked on. Choices


Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser setting to decline cookies by visiting the Help portion of your browser’s toolbar. If accessing this site from a public computer, please be sure to clear the browser cookies when your session is complete.

Flash cookies operate differently than browser cookies, and cookie management tools available in a web browser will not remove flash cookies. To learn more about how to manage flash cookies, you can visit the Adobe website and make changes at the Global Privacy Settings Panel.

Your mobile device may allow you to control cookies through its settings function. Refer to your device manufacturer’s instructions for more information. If you choose to decline cookies, some parts of the EasyTravel Agency website may not work as intended or may not work at all.
