Cookies

Cabovillas.com uses cookies and similar technologies to help provide, protect, and improve the Cabovillas.com website. This policy explains how and why we use these technologies and the choices you have.

A cookie is a small data file that is transferred to your device (e.g. your phone or your computer). For example, a cookie could allow us to recognize your browser, while another could store your recently viewed properties. There are two types of cookies used on the Cabovillas.com website: (1) “session cookies” and (2) “persistent cookies.” Session cookies normally expire when you close your browser, while persistent cookies remain on your device after you close your browser, and can be used again the next time you access the Cabovillas.com website.

We also use other technologies with similar functionality to cookies, such as web beacons, pixels, mobile identifiers, and tracking URLs, to obtain information. For example, our email messages may contain web beacons and tracking URLs to determine whether you have opened a certain message or accessed a certain link.



Why We Uses These Technologies


We use these technologies for a number of purposes, such as:

  1. To enable, facilitate and streamline the functioning of and your access to the Cabovillas.com website.
  2. To better understand how you navigate through and interact with the Cabovillas.com website and to improve the Cabovillas.com website.
  3. To serve you tailored advertising on third party websites.
  4. To monitor and analyze the performance, operation, and effectiveness of the Cabovillas.com website and Cabovillas.com advertisements.
  5. To enforce legal agreements that govern use of the Cabovillas.com website.
  6. For fraud detection and prevention, and investigations.
  7. For purposes of our own customer support, analytics, research, product development, and regulatory compliance.
